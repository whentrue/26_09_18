Splinter is the second full studio album from Sneaker Pimps. Released on October 25, 1999, Splinter is the first release from the band without Kelli Dayton singing lead vocals; she was replaced by founding band member Chris Corner. The sound of the album was a great change from their first album. Most notably it was a darker sound which favored acoustic guitars, dark lyrics, and male vocals.
